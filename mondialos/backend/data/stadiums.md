# Lusail Stadium

Lusail Stadium is the flagship venue with a capacity of 88,966. It hosts the
final and major knockout matches.

- Main entrances: Gate A (North), Gate B (East), Gate C (West), Gate D (South).
- Accessible entrance: Gate D has step-free access and 12 wheelchair bays.
- Restrooms: Located at each gate concourse; accessible toilets near Gate D.
- Food courts: Concourse level 2, Gates A and C. Halal and vegetarian options.
- Parking: 4,000 spaces; pre-book via the MondialOS app. EV charging at Gate B.
- Security checkpoints: All bags screened at outer ring before gate entry.
- Lost & Found: Desk at Gate B concourse, open until 1 hour after final whistle.

# Ibn Battuta Stadium (IKB)

Ibn Battuta Stadium holds 62,400 and is known for its shaded concourses.

- Entrances: North, South, East, West.
- Accessible entrance: South gate, level path from drop-off zone.
- Restrooms: Every concourse; family rooms at North and South.
- Food courts: South concourse (local cuisine focus).
- Parking: 2,200 spaces, first-come first-served.
- Security: Walk-through metal detectors at each gate.
- Lost & Found: North concourse info point.
