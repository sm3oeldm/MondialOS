# Visitor Guide for International Fans

- Language: English and Arabic widely spoken at venues; MondialOS chat supports
  multiple languages.
- Currency: Local currency; card payments preferred.
- SIM / eSIM: Free tourist eSIM available at airport kiosks.
- Emergency number: 112 (police/medical/fire).
- Best local food near Lusail: Souq district, 20 min by metro.
- Family tips: Family zones at Gate D; soft-play before kickoff.
