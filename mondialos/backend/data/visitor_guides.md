# Visitor Guide — FIFA World Cup 2026 (USA / Mexico / Canada)

The 2026 World Cup spans three countries. Plan cross-border travel early.

- Languages: English (USA/Canada), French (Canada), Spanish (Mexico).
  MondialOS chat supports multiple languages.
- Currency: US Dollar (USA), Mexican Peso (Mexico), Canadian Dollar (Canada).
  Card payments preferred; many venues are cashless.
- Cross-border: A valid passport is required between all three countries.
  ESTA/eTA/visa rules apply depending on nationality.
- Emergency numbers: USA 911 · Mexico 911 · Canada 911.
- Getting around: Host cities have metro/rail; cross-city travel between
  venues often requires domestic flights or long drives — book early.
- Family tips: Family zones available at most stadiums; shading and cooling
  stations provided for summer heat (especially USA/Mexico venues).
