# Accessibility Information

- Step-free routes available at MetLife West (MetLife Stadium, NY/NJ) and the
  accessible entry at AT&T Stadium (Dallas). Canada venues (BMO Field, BC Place)
  and Mexico venues (Estadio Azteca, Estadio Akron, Estadio BBVA) provide ramp
  access at main entrances.
- Wheelchair bays must be booked with the ticket.
- Companion seating available on request.
- Sensory bags and quiet spaces at Gate B info desk.
- Accessible toilets at every concourse near the accessible entrance.
- Assistance dogs welcome; water stations provided.
