Note: The full 48-team field is finalized after qualifying concludes. Teams
are grouped into 12 groups of 4 for the expanded format. The top 2 of each
group plus 8 best third-placed teams advance to a 32-team knockout round.

## Format notes
- 104 total matches (up from 64 in 2022).
- Opening match: Estadio Azteca, Mexico City.
- Final: MetLife Stadium, New York/New Jersey, 19 July 2026.
