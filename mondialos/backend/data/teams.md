# Teams (Demo Profiles)

- Morocco: Defensive solidity, rapid wing counters, passionate support.
- Portugal: Possession-heavy, wide overloads, elite finishing.
- Brazil: Jogo bonito, flair midfield, high press when out of possession.
- France: Transition kings, physical midfield, lethal counters.
- Argentina: Low block into vertical passes, Messi-era possession control.
- Japan: Compact shape, disciplined pressing, set-piece efficiency.
