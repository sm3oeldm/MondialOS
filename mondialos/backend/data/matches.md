# FIFA World Cup 2026 — Schedule (Verified Static)

Tournament window: 11 June – 19 July 2026. 104 matches across 16 stadiums
in USA, Mexico, and Canada. This is the published framework; exact kickoff
times are filled in by FIFA nearer the date.

## Key fixtures (confirmed)
- Opening Match: Estadio Azteca, Mexico City — 11 June 2026.
- Final: MetLife Stadium (New York New Jersey Stadium), 19 July 2026.
- Most matches at a single venue: AT&T Stadium / Dallas Stadium (9 matches).

## Group stage
- 12 groups (A–L) of 4 teams each.
- 72 group-stage matches.
- Top 2 per group + 8 best third-placed teams advance (32 teams) to knockout.

## Knockout stage
- Round of 32, Round of 16, Quarter-finals, Semi-finals, Final.
- Third-place playoff between losing semi-finalists.
