# Tournament FAQ

- When do gates open? 3 hours before kickoff.
- Can I bring a bag? Only clear bags up to 30x30x15 cm.
- Cashless? Yes, all stadium payments are card or mobile only.
- Re-entry? No re-entry once you exit the stadium.
- Ticketing issues? Use the MondialOS app chat (Fan Agent) for help.
- Lost ticket? Visit the box office at Gate B with photo ID.
- Prohibited items? Flares, drones, outside food, large umbrellas.
